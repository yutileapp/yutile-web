Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Yutile ("we", "our", or "us"). Yutile is an AI-powered life management app built for individuals and families. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect and use it.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application (Yutile for Android, with iOS coming soon), our website at yutile.com, and all related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Yutile, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📦 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile information — household size, preferences, and settings you configure
  • Content you create — goals, habits, health logs, household tasks, trip plans, notes, and other pillar data
  • Household member information — names and contact details of household members you link within the app
  • Financial information — budget entries and expense records (stored locally on your device; never transmitted to our servers)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage data — features used, session duration, and crash reports (anonymised)
  • Location data — approximate location (city/region level) used only for grocery deal searches, and only when you use that feature

🔍 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Yutile app and its features
  • Power Nova, our AI companion, with context about your life across all pillars
  • Enable household sharing features — tasks, meal plans, events, and shopping lists
  • Send you reminders, notifications, and updates you have configured
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Analyse usage patterns (in aggregate, anonymised form) to improve the product
  • Send you product updates and announcements (you can opt out at any time)

💾 Data Storage

Yutile is designed with a local-first approach. The majority of your personal data — goals, habits, health logs, finances, and notes — is stored directly on your device.

Data that is synced to our servers (Firebase, operated by Google) includes:

  • Your account credentials and profile
  • Data you explicitly share with household members (shared tasks, events, shopping lists)
  • Push notification tokens for reminders

Our servers are located in the United States and Canada. If you are located outside these regions, your data may be transferred to and processed in these countries.

🤝 Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Household members you link — shared data (tasks, events, shopping lists) is visible to linked household members within Yutile
  • Service providers — we use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Claude (Anthropic) for AI features. These providers process data only as instructed by us and in accordance with our agreements with them
  • Grocery ordering — when you choose to order groceries or household items, the names of the items on your shopping list are sent to our grocery-ordering partner to generate a shopping cart on their platform. We do not share any other personal information for this purpose
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers — if Yutile is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction
We do not display ads. We do not share your data with advertisers. Ever.

🔒 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS
  • Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication
  • Financial data (budgets, expenses) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data using commercially reasonable means.

👶 Children's Privacy

Yutile is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

⚖️ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@yutile.com or visit our Account Deletion page.

🍪 Cookies and Tracking

Our website (yutile.com) uses minimal cookies necessary for the site to function (such as remembering your waitlist status). We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We use anonymised analytics to understand how features are used and to improve the product.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending an email to your registered address. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

Your continued use of Yutile after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@yutile.com
  • Website: yutile.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
