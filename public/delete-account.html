Account Management

Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your Yutile account at any time — here's how.

📱 Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from the Yutile app:

1

Open Yutile

Launch the app on your Android device

2

Go to Profile

Tap your profile icon in the top right corner of the home screen

3

Open Settings

Scroll down and tap "Settings" or the gear icon

4

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and tap "Delete Account"

5

Confirm deletion

You'll be asked to confirm. Once confirmed, your account and data are permanently deleted

📧 Request via Email

If you can't access the app or prefer to request deletion by email, contact us at:

📧 Email us to delete your account

Please include the email address associated with your Yutile account. We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation.

⚠️ What Gets Deleted

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

When you delete your account, the following is permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • All goals, habits, health logs, and personal pillar data
  • Your household links and shared content you created
  • Your trip plans, packing lists, and itineraries
  • All Nova AI conversation history
  • Your waitlist or beta registration

Note: Content you shared with household members (tasks, events, shopping lists) may remain visible to those members until they delete it from their own accounts.

💡 Before You Go

If you're leaving because of a problem or missing feature, we'd love to hear from you. Your feedback directly shapes Yutile's development.

Share feedback with our team →

If you just need a break, you can simply stop using the app — you don't need to delete your account. Your data will be waiting when you return.

📬 Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact our support team:

  • Email: support@yutile.com
  • Privacy: privacy@yutile.com

We aim to respond within 2 business days.
