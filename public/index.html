Everything connected

Nova turns plans into
action, instantly

From meal plans to mindset goals, Yutile connects every pillar of your life to real-world action — so nothing stays just a plan.

🍽️
Meal Plans → Groceries
Build your weekly meal plan and convert it to a grocery list with one tap. Choose delivery at home or pickup near your office — wherever works for you.
👨‍👩‍👧
Shared Household Lists
Linked household members share one live shopping list. Anyone can fulfil it — dad picks up on the way home, mum orders delivery. Coordinated effortlessly.
🎉
Events → Shopping
Planning a birthday or dinner party? Nova auto-generates a shopping list based on your event and guest count, ready to order in one tap.
🎁
Household Wishlists
Save items to your household wishlist for special occasions. Any household member can convert the wishlist to an order — delivered or picked up from a nearby store.
💜
Health Goals → Supplements
Set a health goal and Nova suggests relevant vitamins, supplements, and wellness products — curated to your goal and ready to order with one tap.
🧘
Mindset → Wellness
Tracking stress, sleep, or meditation goals? Nova recommends herbal teas, aromatherapy, and relaxation essentials — delivered to your door.
🔄
Habits → Auto-reorder
Have a daily habit like taking vitamins or drinking green tea? Nova schedules recurring orders so you never run out — your habits, fully automated.
✦
Nova connects it all
Every pillar in Yutile is connected. Nova sees the full picture and turns your plans, goals, and events into real action — automatically.
